# AniScope Design System

## Brand
- Name: AniScope
- Meaning: Anime + Eye + Scope
- Tagline: See your next masterpiece.

## Colors
- Background: #07080d
- Panel: #11131b
- Purple: #9754ff
- Orange: #ff7a2a
- Green: #3ee095
- Gold: #ffd06a

## Principles
1. Recommendation-first, not database-first.
2. Every recommendation explains why it fits.
3. Mobile-first and iPhone-safe.
4. Poster art must feel intentional and consistent.
5. Keep pages skimmable; avoid walls of text.
